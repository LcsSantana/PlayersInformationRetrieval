    If you wonder how to check Over 1.5 goals bet? The answer is very simple. Bet over 1.5 is correct if the sum of goals of both teams in the match was 2 or more. Bet under 1.5 is valid if the sum of goals of both teams in the match is 0 or 1 goals.
