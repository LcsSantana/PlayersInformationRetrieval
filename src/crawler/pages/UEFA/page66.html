Inter, Dortmund among first winners

Tuesday 10 September 2019

Article summary

Inter, Dortmund, Lyon, Benfica and Ajax secured wins on Tuesday.

Article top media content

Inter via Getty Images

Article body

 

  • Group winners progress to round of 16
  • Group runners-up enter play-offs v eight Domestic Champions path winners for round of 16 slots
  • All matches
  • Where to watch
  • 4 games a week streamed on UEFA.tv in selected territories

Matchday one

Tuesday 17 September
Group E: Napoli 1-1 Liverpool (highlights to follow), Salzburg 1-1 Genk 
Group F: Inter 4-0 Slavia Praha, Dortmund 2-1 Barcelona (highlights to follow)
Group G: Lyon 4-2 Zenit, Benfica 2-1 Leipzig 
Group H: Chelsea 3-3 Valencia, Ajax 4-0 LOSC Lille

Wednesday 18 September
Group A: Club Brugge v Galatasaray, Paris v Real Madrid (UEFA.tv)
Group B: Olympiacos v Tottenham, Bayern v Crvena zvezda 
Group C: Shakhtar v Manchester City, Dinamo Zagreb v Atalanta 
Group D: Atlético v Juventus (UEFA.tv), Leverkusen v Lokomotiv Moskva

Matchday two

Tuesday 1 October
Group A: Real Madrid v Club Brugge, Galatasaray v Paris 
Group B: Tottenham v Bayern (UEFA.tv), Crvena zvezda v Olympiacos 
Group C: Atalanta v Shakhtar, Manchester City v Dinamo Zagreb
Group D: Juventus v Leverkusen (UEFA.tv), Lokomotiv Moskva v Atlético 

Wednesday 2 October
Group E: Genk v Napoli, Liverpool v Salzburg 
Group F: Slavia Praha v Dortmund, Barcelona v Inter (UEFA.tv)
Group G: Zenit v Benfica, Leipzig v Lyon 
Group H: Valencia v Ajax, LOSC Lille v Chelsea (UEFA.tv)

Matchday three

Tuesday 22 October
Group A: Club Brugge v Paris ﻿(UEFA.tv), Galatasaray v Real Madrid
Group B: Tottenham v Crvena zvezda, Olympiacos v Bayern 
Group C: Shakhtar v Dinamo Zagreb, Manchester City v Atalanta
Group D: Atlético v Leverkusen (UEFA.tv), Juventus v Lokomotiv Moskva 

Wednesday 23 October
Group E: Salzburg v Napoli, Genk v Liverpool 
Group F: Inter v Dortmund (UEFA.tv), Slavia Praha v Barcelona 
Group G: Leipzig v Zenit, Benfica v Lyon 
Group H: Ajax v Chelsea (UEFA.tv), LOSC Lille v Valencia 

Matchday four

Tuesday 5 November
Group E: Liverpool v Genk, Napoli v Salzburg 
Group F: Barcelona v Slavia Praha, Dortmund v Inter (UEFA.tv)
Group G: Zenit v Leipzig, Lyon v Benfica (UEFA.tv)
Group H: Chelsea v Ajax, Valencia v LOSC Lille 

Wednesday 6 November
Group A: Paris v Club Brugge, Real Madrid v Galatasaray (UEFA.tv)
Group B: Bayern v Olympiacos, Crvena zvezda v Tottenham 
Group C: Dinamo Zagreb v Shakhtar, Atalanta v Manchester City (UEFA.tv)
Group D: Lokomotiv Moskva v Juventus, Leverkusen v Atlético 

Matchday five

Tuesday 26 November
Group A: Galatasaray v Club Brugge, Real Madrid v Paris 
Group B: Tottenham v Olympiacos, Crvena zvezda v Bayern 
Group C: Manchester City v Shakhtar, Atalanta v Dinamo Zagreb 
Group D: Lokomotiv Moskva v Leverkusen, Juventus v Atlético 

Wednesday 27 November
Group E: Liverpool v Napoli, Genk v Salzburg 
Group F: Barcelona v Dortmund, Slavia Praha v Inter 
Group G: Zenit v Lyon, Leipzig v Benfica 
Group H: Valencia v Chelsea, LOSC Lille v Ajax

Tuesday 10 December
Group E: Napoli v Genk, Salzburg v Liverpool
Group F: Dortmund v Slavia Praha, Inter v Barcelona

Group H: Chelsea v LOSC Lille, Ajax v Valencia 

Wednesday 11 December
Group A: Paris v Galatasaray, Club Brugge v Real Madrid
Group B: Bayern v Tottenham, Olympiacos v Crvena zvezda
