Where to watch the UEFA Youth League

Thursday 12 September 2019

Article summary

See where to watch selected matches in the UEFA Youth League on TV around the world.

Article top media content

©Sportsfile

Article body

Fans can find their local UEFA Youth League broadcast partner(s) below.

  • Where rights are not sold, four group matches a week are streamed by UEFA.tv with highlights available in all territories.
