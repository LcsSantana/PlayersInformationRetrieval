    LiveWomen's Futsal EURO 2019 at a glance

    Spain won the first UEFA Women's Futsal EURO while Vanessa Sotelo was named player of the tournament.

    LiveFinal highlights: Spain claim the crown

    Spain beat hosts Portugal 4-0 in Gondomar to win the inaugural UEFA Women's Futsal EURO.

    LiveHighlights: Russia pip Ukraine for bronze

    Russia beat Ukraine 3-2 on penalties for bronze having earlier seen a 2-0 lead wiped out in nornal time.
